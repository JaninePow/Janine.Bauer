About, in brief


Timo Bohnstedt is a recent graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems and Management from the Nuremberg Institute of Technology. His ultimate career goal is to solve a problem affecting over 10^9 people, particularly in the fields of Healthcare and engineering. Additional to his work in urban mobility, Timo has involved in the Beiersdorf Battelweeks Hackathon, research at Pattern Recognition Lab University Erlangen Nuremberg, as well as Kaggle challenges. Currently, a Computer Science Masters student, Timo is also working in fields of clinical data science, machine learning for biology, and deep learning for archology.
